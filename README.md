# sandbox-repo

Literature review on community based targeting specifically and social protection in sahel more generally
Create a spreadsheet with the following information: 
1) name of the paper
2) authors
3) url/link to paper
4) methodology
5) data used
6) key findings
7) what gap it tries to address
8) what gaps it highlights that still exist

Key bodies of work you would want to engage include: Alatas et al. (2012) on community targeting in Indonesia, the broader CBT debate in the Journal of Development Economics and World Development, and literature on social protection in the Sahel specifically. 

This literature review will feed into a report/paper on the use of community based targeting in Mali. The work focuses on the committee dilemma under near-universal poverty — the "managing the impossible" dynamic where selection ratios are so low relative to actual vulnerability that criteria-based targeting becomes socially and practically incoherent. Keep this in mind when selecting the papers for this literature review. 

I would also need some literature on the following frameworks:
1) principal-agent theory (project designers vs. committee implementers),
2) Lipsky's street-level bureaucracy concept
3) the social capital literature on community institutions.

Instead of conducting the entire lit review all at once. Review a paper and add details to the spreadsheet before starting the next paper.
